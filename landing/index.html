The Problem

The Price Was Never The Problem

Real quotes for the exact same 45-mile move, same apartment, same service — nothing hidden, nothing different. Just whoever answered the phone.

$1,158 → $6,506

A 5.6× spread for identical work. Not a data problem — a time problem. Nobody has the hours to call eight companies, compare fine print, and push back on hidden fees. So most people take the friendliest quote, and hope. FMCSA data shows that hope is wrong 40% of the time.

How It Works

Three Modules, One Loop

Sourcing, screening, and negotiation — closed end to end, evidence at every step.

01 — THE ESTIMATOR

Intake by Voice

A voice interview builds one confirmed job spec — origin, destination, stairs, large items — reused word-for-word on every call after. Closes the sight-unseen gap that causes most bad estimates.

02 — THE CALLER

Real Negotiation

Phones the market, describes the job identically every time, and extracts itemized quotes across distinct styles — the stonewaller, the lowballer, the tough negotiator.

03 — THE CLOSER

Leverage & Verdict

Uses earlier quotes as real leverage mid-call, flags anything suspiciously cheap instead of celebrating it, and reports a ranked, cited recommendation.
