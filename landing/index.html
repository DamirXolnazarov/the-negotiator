THE PROBLEM

Real quotes for the exact same 45-mile move ranged from $1,158 to $6,506. Not a data problem — a time problem. Nobody has the hours to call eight companies, compare fine print, and push back on hidden fees. So most people just take the friendliest quote, and hope.

THREE MODULES, ONE LOOP

01

The Estimator

A voice interview builds one confirmed job spec — reused word for word on every call, closing the sight-unseen gap that causes most bad estimates.

02

The Caller

Phones the market, describes the job identically every time, and extracts itemized quotes from real negotiation — stonewallers, lowballers, tough negotiators.

03

The Closer

Uses earlier quotes as real leverage mid-call, flags anything suspiciously cheap instead of celebrating it, and reports a ranked, cited recommendation.

SEE THE PROOF

Not a claim — a live trace of the negotiation, the honesty check, and the final verdict.
